Your Vehicle Shipping Questions Answered

How much does vehicle shipping cost from dealer-to-dealer?

Depending on the vehicle’s size and weight, distance as well as number of cars in one load will depend on a vehicle shipping rate from dealer to dealer.

How do I find the best auto transport broker for dealers?

It is important to ensure that the transport broker is fully licensed, bonded as well as insured. Many brokers have licenses and bonds but do not have broker insurance. ShipCar24 is offering comprehensive insurance coverage for brokers that covers dealership and auction buyer’s vehicles while they are in transit.

How do I prepare a vehicle for shipping from dealership to a customer?

It is recommended that vehicles are clean and detailed before shipping them to the customers. This helps the driver to conduct more thorough inspection when he picks up the vehicle. We require drivers to take photos on the pickup and delivery to ensure it arrives in preexisting condition.

Do I need to fuel up the vehicle before shipping from dealership to customer?

Car transport standard is to have not more than ¼ of the fuel tank of gas, however, this is subject to negotiation with the driver.

Are my vehicles insured when shipped as a full load?

Unlike many other brokers and carriers, when you ship your full loads with us from VPS other dealerships, we make sure they are shipped in combination that doesn’t exceed carrier’s insurance limit. We choose not to risk our customers’ vehicles to ensure safe and fully insured deliveries each time.

What are contractual obligations to sign up with auto shipping brokers?

As of right now ShipCar24 is offering obligation free and contract free shipping for dealerships, auction buyers, government agencies, armoring companies, vehicle manufacturers, ports, moving companies and other businesses.

What are the benefits of shipping a car through a car shipping broker?

Knowledgeable and experienced car shipping brokers such as ShipCar24 will ensure that dealer’s a vehicle is shipped with licensed and insured carrier. Due to increasing fraud, car theft, and car damage, it is important to ensure that every vehicle is transported with a reliable carrier. After shipping hundreds of thousands of vehicles for dealers and private individuals ShipCar24 offers extensive expertise that any business can rely on. Our strictest carrier selection policy has eliminated “ghost” carriers “paper on the window” carriers that may potentially cause harm to our clients.

Why use vehicle shipping broker instead of carrier directly?

While using a car carrier directly may save dealer or auction buyer a few dollars, it will be hard to track if the carrier is fully licensed and compliant to operate on the public highway. In addition, their insurance certificate may be counterfeit or insurance policy may be pending cancellation or inactive. As a car transport broker ShipCar24 has tools to check and monitor carrier’s insurance and licensing every time they dispatch a vehicle to them. In addition, ShipCar24 is a fully insured broker which guarantees safe vehicle delivery.
